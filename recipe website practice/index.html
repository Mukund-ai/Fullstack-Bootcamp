Today's Special Recipes

Authentic Indian dishes made with love and spice!

Ingredients (Paneer Butter Masala)

  • 200g Paneer cubes
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Fry onions and tomatoes, blend to paste.
  2. Add butter and masala, cook 5 mins.
  3. Add paneer cubes and simmer 10 mins.
  4. Garnish with cream and serve hot.

Ingredients (Veg Biryani)

  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp Biryani masala
  • 1 Onion (fried)
  • Ghee & salt to taste

Method

  1. Cook rice 80% and keep aside.
  2. Sauté veggies and masala in ghee.
  3. Layer rice and veggies in a pan.
  4. Cover and cook 10 mins on low flame.

Ingredients (Gulab Jamun)

  • 1 cup Khoya
  • 2 tbsp Maida
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 cups Water
  • Oil for frying

Method

  1. Make soft dough using khoya and maida.
  2. Shape small balls and fry until golden.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup and soak jamuns.
  4. Serve warm with love.
